CyberAware is a training program that will help students and teachers gain the education and knowledge they need to defend against threats and understand security standards. CyberAware aims to provide an understanding to protect the school's infrastructure. This product prepares our clients with cybersecurity basics, phishing awareness, password security, privacy issues, network security, monitoring system, and training. They will be able to point out what a cyber-attack is, what a phishing email looks like, how to create strong passwords, learn how to minimize risks, etc. Our goal is to raise Cybersecurity Awareness.
